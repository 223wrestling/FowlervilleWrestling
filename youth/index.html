About the Program

The Fowlerville Youth Wrestling Club is open to all students in grades K-8. Our program focuses on teaching proper wrestling fundamentals in a safe, encouraging environment. Whether your child is brand new to wrestling or has years of experience, our coaching staff will help them develop skills and confidence.

Wrestlers will learn stance and motion, takedowns, top and bottom position techniques, and the basics of chain wrestling. We emphasize sportsmanship, hard work, and having fun.

What to Expect

1

Warm-Up & Drills

Each practice starts with stretching, jogging, and wrestling-specific drills to build coordination.

2

Technique Instruction

Coaches demonstrate and break down moves step-by-step, then wrestlers practice with partners.

3

Live Wrestling

Practice ends with situational and live wrestling so kids can apply what they learned.
