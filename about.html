About Fowlerville Wrestling

Building champions on and off the mat.

Our Program

Fowlerville Wrestling serves the Fowlerville, Michigan community with wrestling programs for youth (grades K-8) and high school athletes. Our mission is to develop well-rounded wrestlers who compete with intensity, display sportsmanship, and carry the lessons of the sport into their daily lives.

We emphasize fundamental technique, chain wrestling, and a strong work ethic. Every wrestler - from first-year beginners to varsity competitors - is part of the Fowlerville wrestling family.

Our Programs

Youth Club (K-8)

Our youth program introduces wrestlers to the sport with an emphasis on fundamentals, fun, and fitness. Practices are held twice a week November through February. No experience required.

Learn More

High School Varsity & JV

The Fowlerville Gladiators compete in MHSAA Division 3. Our high school program builds on the fundamentals taught in the youth program and prepares athletes for varsity-level competition.
